HARDWARE

Hover Over Any Hardware In The Computer

HARDDRIVE:

MOTHERBOARD: This is the main Printed Circuit Board (PCB) that contains and allows all communication and interaction between the principal components of a system, such being the CPU, RAM, and graphics card slots. It is also reffered to as the mainframe or body of a system.

BUS: Those thin lines all over the motherboard are bus, simple circuits that connect one part of the motherboard to another .

CENTRAL PROCESSING UNIT (CPU): Commonly referred to as the "backbone" or "brain" of a computer, this is where instructions sent from components and programs that are calculated using basic arithmetic, then processed to enable the system to perform functions.
