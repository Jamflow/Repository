Vindinium is a game that utilized player-programmed artificial intelligence designed to simulate the interaction between AI’s. These AI's are displayed as heroes that compete to gather the most gold for a set number of turns. Through these turns, heroes can face blockades of trees, encounter other AI’s, and claim mines in the scattered throughout the generated map. Once the turns end, the bot with the most gold harvested from the mines wins and will stand as the dominating AI.
