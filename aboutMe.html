Who am I?

My name is Jamar Pagpaguitan, but I also goes by JP. I'm currently a Highschool senior attending Summit Shasta Public School in Daly City, California. As far as my life has gone, I have only travelled domestically in the United States, including Las Vegas, Hawai'i, Florida, Seattle, Washington, and all over California. I was born in San Francisco, but I've moved all over the Bay Area, Las Vegas, and finally settling in South San Francisco by the start of Junior High. Living in one of the most multiculturally diverse places in the world, I'm Filipino.

I own Summit Shasta's Film Club along with senior Jonathan Rodriguez. Some of the things I love include film, art, boxing, sailing, coding, drawing, and construction. In particular, I am presently pursuing art and boxing.

From art, it's been a hobby for me to draw as I've been drawing since the age of 3 and like to express my perspective, issues, and life. I love drawing in general, from portraits to buildings and symbols to murals.

After highschool, I would like to attend California State University Maritime Academy and major in Facilities Engineering Technology or otherwise simply Mechanical Engineering in another affordable and beneficial in-state college.
